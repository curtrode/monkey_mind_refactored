Please evaluate the following:

## Consistency
- Do the proposal and portfolio tell the same story? Are there contradictions in terminology, phase names, technical claims, or framing?
- Does the video match what the portfolio and proposal describe?
- Are all links functional and pointing to the right destinations?

## Alignment with program expectations
- Does the proposal clearly articulate what work will be done during the residency (vs. what already exists)?
- Does the portfolio demonstrate sufficient technical and literary competence for a computational writing program?
- Is the lineage section convincing — does the applicant show awareness of the field without overclaiming?
- Is the proposed residency work (multi-model dialogue) a credible next step from the current prototype?

## Gaps or weaknesses
- Is anything unclear, missing, or unconvincing?
- Would a reviewer unfamiliar with electronic literature understand the project?
- Does the application distinguish the applicant's creative/authorial role from the LLM's generative role?

Please be direct and specific in your feedback.
